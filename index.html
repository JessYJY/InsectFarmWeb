ABOUT

This project aims to produce mobile and cost-effective rendering plant, capable of processing insects into meals and oils that meet regulatory requirements. The goal for this semester is to finish the designing part of the project.

Audit 1

  • Establish landing page and repository
  • Clarify the ojectives to define project
  • Complete the 1st of Concept of Operation

    • Audit 2

  • Complete engineering governance
  • Research on the plant design
  • Generate and sketch design schemes
  • Update Concept of Operation

    • Audit 3

  • Finalise the plant design
  • Validate and evaluate the design
  • Complete the handover documents

    Milestone

    • 2 Aug - 9 Aug

      Define Project

      Communicate with client to clarify the project scope and requirements and learn about the existing issues.

    • 10 Aug - 13 Aug

      Research

      Research on the current technologies of the exiting food processing plant and their physical designs.

    • 14 Aug - 23 Aug

      Idea Generation

      Following the systematic engineering procedures, brainstone on the design ideas, generate several possible design schemes and choose the best solutions.

    • 24 Aug - 13 Sep

      First Design

      Complete the first version of the detailed design with components in each sections identified and gain the feedback from client, third party supervisors and shadow team.

    • 14 Sep - 27 Sep

      Design Optimisation

      Optimise the initial design with the feedback.

    • 28 Sep - 4 Oct

      Validation and Evaluation

      Validate and evaluate the design achieved the project requirements.

    • 5 Oct - 11 Oct

      Project Finalisation

      Finalise the design and documentate all the information of the project back to client.
